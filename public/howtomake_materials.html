Materials Needed

Here is the list of materials and tools that you'll need in order to make your own nylon whip:

  • A bundle of 1/4 in. diameter White Braided Nylon Rope

    (NOTE: The overall length needed will depend on how long you want your finished whip to be. See the Cutting Your Rope section to determine how much rope you'll need.)

  • At least 5 ft. of Twisted Mason Line
  • Tape Measurer
  • A Lighter (preferably a butane lighter)
  • Scissors
  • Needle Nose Plyers
  • Forceps or a Mini Binder Clip
  • One roll of Grip Tape or Electrical Tape
  • A Clamp or a heavy Soft-Cover Book (in case you need to step away from your work and don't want it coming undone)
