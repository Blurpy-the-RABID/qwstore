Welcome To Quick Whips!

The art of whip cracking has been around for quite a long time. In recent years whip cracking has been made popular due to film and television. Indiana Jones, Zorro, and Catwoman are just three popular characters that come to most people's minds when they think about whip cracking. Perhaps these characters have inspired you to pursue the art of whip cracking. But then you saw how much a standard leather or paracord whip will cost, and you may become discouraged.

Fear not! There is a much cheaper and equally efficient way to get yourself a whip to practice with! You will find exactly what you need here at Quick Whips! This site will show you how to make your own whip out of nylon rope, which is easily found at your local hardware store. This process takes very little time to complete, so it won't take long for you to get to the fun part - actually cracking a whip! If you're not interested in making your own whip, then you may contact me so that I can do the work for you!


Why Use A Nylon Whip?

Using a simple whip made of nylon rope has several advantages for novice whip crackers and experts alike! Here's a list of several valid reasons that anyone interested in whip cracking should consider before purchasing their first whip:

  • Nylon whips require much less time to make than a traditional round-braided whip.
  • Nylon whips require far less materials than a round-braided whip of equal (or even shorter) length.
  • Nylon whips can be washed much more easily than a leather whip.
  • Nylon whips can be dyed any color you wish! Customize your whip's appearance and show it off with pride!
  • Nylon is naturally more resistant to most harsh environmental conditions, so maintenance is almost non-existent!
  • If your nylon whip somehow gets destroyed or ruined, it's of little loss to you! Just make a new one and you're good to go!

Explore this site to learn more about what you'll need to create your very own nylon whip! Be safe and have fun!
